ABOUT

A6TH BAND

A6TH, founded in 2019 by Sithija Miranda, has evolved from its humble beginnings as a wedding band to become a versatile musical ensemble renowned for its electrifying performances across Sri Lanka. Weathering the challenges posed by the pandemic, the band underwent a revitalization in 2023, infusing new energy and talent into its lineup. With a repertoire spanning Sinhala, English, pop, audiences at weddings, corporate events, private functions, and popular pubs alike. What sets A6TH apart is not just their exceptional musical prowess, but also their unwavering commitment to delivering unforgettable experiences for their audiences. Equipped with their own professional sound system and a talented team of sound engineers, A6TH ensures that every performance is not only musically captivating but also acoustically impeccable, making them a standout presence in Sri Lanka's vibrant music scene


OUR SERVICES & EVENTS

Experience the electrifying energy of our band as we deliver performances that exceed expectations every time. With our unwavering commitment to quality, we ensure that every gig we play is nothing short of extraordinary, offering a 200% value for the price we charge. Book us for your next event and elevate the entertainment to unforgettable heights!






OUR SOUND SYSTEM

A6TH SOUNDGEAR is more than just a sound system; it's the heartbeat of our band's sonic journey. With the latest, top-of-the-line equipment from around the globe, our sound system delivers crystal-clear audio that sets the stage on fire. What makes our sound truly unique is the expertise of our sound engineer, Susith Prasanna. With a wealth of experience working alongside some of Sri Lanka's biggest names in music, including Black, Nadeeka Guruge, Priya Sooriyasena, W.D. Amaradewa, Lasantha Kariyawasam, Kanchana Sitar, Sampath Anglia, Nirosha Virajini, Senanayaka Weraliyadda, Athula Adikari, Rookantha, and many more, Susith ensures that every performance is a sonic masterpiece. From our newly updated digital audio console to our cutting-edge line array system, A6TH SOUNDGEAR guarantees an unforgettable auditory experience that leaves audiences wanting more
